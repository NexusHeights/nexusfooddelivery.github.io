Benefits

Some of the reasons Nexus Food Delivery can benefit you and the Cookeville community.

Great Team

Nexus Food Delivery is developed by an excellent, capable team of engineers consisting of former Tennessee Tech students.

Providing Jobs

As demand for Nexus Food Delivery grows, more drivers and administrative staff will be needed, providing jobs to the citizens of the Cookeville, TN area.

Clean, Minimalistic Design

Great care has been taken in making sure that Nexus Food Delivery's design remains clear, crisp, and consistent among all of our apps.

Start Up

By using Nexus Food Delivery, you are supporting a local start-up that is staffed and owned entirely by Cookeville, TN residents.

Lower Cost

Nexus Food Delivery is priced lower than competitors, as of July 5th, 2018. All fees are presented to you as clearly as possible, so there are no surprises.

Driver Tracking

Impatience while you wait for food happens to everyone. That's why Nexus Food Delivery shows the location of your driver while they're delivering.

Detailed Order History

Want to know what you ordered 3 months ago and which card you used? Nexus Food Delivery shows you that information and more, for every order.

Consistently Updated

Nexus Food Delivery's mobile and web apps will strive to support new hardware and operating system features shortly after they release.
