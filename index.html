How To Place an Order

Placing an order with Nexus Food Delivery is very simple! The only thing you will need to do is call or text 931-588-1455 and tell us what you would like to eat.

You will then be told the order total before the order is confirmed, so you have a chance to change your mind if you decide not to use it.

Next, you will simply need to give the confirmation and tell us where you want the food to be taken and your name. A driver will then be sent out to the location you provided with your food! It's as simple as that!

Pricing

Nexus Food Delivery has a base delivery fee of $4.00 which makes us the most affordable option in Cookeville!

If you're paying with a card, there is an extra $2.50 charge to cover the cost of processing the card payment.

If you're paying with cash, you only pay the $4.00 delivery fee as there is no service fee.

If you want delivery from more than one restaurant in a single order, there is a $2.00 fee for each additional restaurant in the order. This goes entirely to the driver and helps them cover the cost of transportation.

Delivery Radius

Nexus Food Delivery delivers to any address within a 7 mile radius of the geographical center of Cookeville, TN. This covers the entirety of city limits and most of Algood.

Deliveries outside this radius are handled on a case-by-case basis and may incur an extra $2.50 fee depending on the distance outside the radius.

If you are in doubt about whether or not you can get deliveries from us, please give us a call or text at the number above. In the future, our mobile app will calculate this automatically.

Tipping Drivers

Drivers are paid a 50/50 split of the profit that Nexus Food Delivery makes. That said, we still encourage you to leave a tip for your driver if they provided you with excellent service.

If you are paying with a card, you will have the option when placing an order whether or not to leave a tip on the card payment. Please be aware that a tip cannot be applied to a card after the delivery has been made.

Tipping is entirely optional and it is up to you whether or not you want to leave a tip.
