Nexus Food Delivery Tennessee Tech Limited Launch

We are currently in a limited launch period. What this basically means is we are "testing the waters" to make sure everything runs smoothly in preparation for a full launch while our mobile apps and website are awaiting publication. We will have a limited selection of restaurants during this time, while the full launch will support over 40 restaurants in the Cookeville and Algood areas.

Thank you.

Please dial-in to (423)-438-3198 to place your order. We accept cash and cards. Card charges made through Nexus Food Delivery will show up on your billing statement as Nexus Heights.

The delivery fee associated with this service is $4.00, plus a service fee of $2.50. You will be charged $6.50 over the base cost of the order, plus tax. Please remember to leave your driver a tip!

As an added bonus, using Nexus Food Delivery during this time will entitle you to a 50% off delivery fee coupon during the full launch, as well as a free magnet. Please send us an email at promotion@nexusfooddelivery.com with the phone number you used to place an order so we can get it to you.
