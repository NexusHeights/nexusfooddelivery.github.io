Nexus Food Delivery Limited Launch

We are currently in a limited launch period. What this basically means is we are "testing the waters" to make sure everything runs smoothly in preparation for a full launch while our mobile apps and website are awaiting publication. We will have a limited selection of restaurants during this time, while the full launch will support over 40 restaurants in the Cookeville and Algood areas. Thank you!

Please call or text (423)-438-3198 to place your order today!

If you are submitting your order via text and using a card, please do not submit your card details over text! Provide them to the driver when your food is delivered so he/she can enter them into our secure payment processor. Thank you!


Pricing

Nexus Food Delivery has the best prices in town, guaranteed! For only a $4.00 delivery fee and a $2.50 service/processing fee if you pay with a card, you can get hot and fresh food delivered to your home, business, or dorm room.

Buying for a group? Nexus Food Delivery charges a $4.00 delivery fee no matter the size of the order, whether it is a $10 order or a $100 order. However, please be aware that there is an extra $2.00 charge for every additional restaurant after one that you want food delivered from to help our drivers cover the cost of their transportation.

Discounts and Free Stuff

Using Nexus Food Delivery during this limited launch period entitles you to receive a coupon code for 50% off the delivery fee to be used during the full launch. Just send us an email at promotion@nexusfooddelivery.com with the phone number you used to place an order and we will send you your code!

You will also receive a free Nexus Food Delivery magnet with your order.

Scheduling Orders

Need food delivered on a specific date and time? Let us know, and we can get it to you at the exact time you need it. This service is provided at no additional charge.

We only ask that scheduled orders be submitted 2 hours in advance.

Estimated Order Times

We have an estimate of 30-45 minutes for all orders during the limited launch period.

These times may fluctuate depending on your distance to the restaurant, how busy the restaurant is, traffic, and more. If your estimated order time is expected to be longer than 30-45 minutes, we will let you know ahead of time before you place your order.

Driver Availability

Demand for Nexus Food Delivery may sometimes exceed driver availability. We will let you know ahead of time if there is going to be a delay due to all of our drivers being on a delivery.
